class filemanager.domain.uploads.lock.LockState[source]¶

Bases: enum.Enum

Upload workspace lock states.

LOCKED = 'LOCKED'¶

Indicates upload workspace is locked and cannot be updated.

The workspace might be locked during publish or when admins are updating uploaded files.

UNLOCKED = 'UNLOCKED'¶

Workspace is unlocked, updates are allowed.

Workspace is normally in this state.
